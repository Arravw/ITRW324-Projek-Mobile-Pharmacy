About Us

This site is a platform, to enable people to order medicine, when they are not able to go and get it at a pharmacy nearby. The ordered medicine will be delivered at the address of the person, which ordered it. Our pharmacy have highly recommended staff.

Our head office is located in Gerrit Dekker Street, Potchefstroom, North West, 2520.

Few main points of our service:

  • Providing consultation.
  • Dispensing of products.
  • Keeping our customers happy.

Practice Areas

Red Cross Mobile Pharmacy has highly recommended expierenced professional staff. We have aquired these professionals from different parts of the world.

Main practice area

The different practice areas include the following:

  • Clinical research associate
  • Medical sales representative
  • Pharmacologist
  • Inpatient Pharmacist
  • Chemotherapy Pharmacist

Clinical research associate

They focus on monitoring and running clinical trails.

Medical sales representative

Their job description is to persuade doctors to recommended their medicine to patients.

Pharmacologist

Conducting the needed research to ensure that the medicine work for the specific target group.

Inpatient Pharmacist

They are responsible for providing services to patients that are on atleast one type of medicine.

Chemotherapy Pharmacist

Responsible for preparing the chemotherapy drugs for the patients, and to ensure that they get it.

Services

The different services that are provided by Red Cross Mobile Pharmacy are:

Delivery of products
